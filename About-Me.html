I am a Ist year undergraduate student, studying Computer Science and Engineering at IIT Roorkee.

I have a deep interest in algorithms & problem solving. Also, i hold a desire to learn new tech-related stuff and develop useful applications. As a result of my interests, i am a member of two of the most innovative groups on campus- SDSLabs and PAG.


SDSLabs is a student group which aims at the promotion of various technical activities within the IIT Roorkee campus. SDSLabs members constantly strive to innovate and ship awesome applications to the people of IITR.


PAG - Programming and Algorithms group - is a group which strives to promote the "coding culture" in IITR. PAG hosts regular mathematical and algorithmic contests, and conducts lectures to promote algorithms within the campus.
